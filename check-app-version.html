How to Check Your App Version

Follow these 3 simple steps to find the current version of your Digital Signage Solution app on Windows.

01
🖥️
Open the App

Launch the Digital Signage Solution application as you normally would. The app will start in fullscreen mode by default.

02
⌨️
Press ESC to Exit Fullscreen

While the app is running, press ESC on your keyboard. The app will exit fullscreen mode and display a standard window with a top bar.

📡
Digital Signage Solution - v1.5.7
app content area
💡 The version number is shown in the top-left of the title bar, formatted as v[number] (e.g. Digital Signage Solution – v1.5.7).
03
↩️
Press F11 to Go Back to Fullscreen

Once you've noted the version number, press F11 to return the app to fullscreen mode and resume normal operation.

ESC → View version → F11 → Back to fullscreen
